Pulka-Paneer Butter Masala

Description:

This Paneer Butter Masala recipe is a rich and creamy dish of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a tomato, butter and cashew sauce that is known here as “makhani gravy.” The acidity of the tomatoes and the sweetness of the cream make for a velvety, nearly addictive sauce.I know, I know, the thought of me cooking with meat sounds insane. It seems nearly a lifetime ago!
