Chicken Lollipop

Description:

Chicken lollipop is one of the most loved chicken wings starters. It is essentially a recipe for crispy batter-fried chicken wings made using chicken drumettes and wingettes. The inside of these fried chicken wings is juicy and flavorful, while the exterior is superbly crispy. Served with spicy schezwan sauce or sweet chilli garlic dip, its heavenly taste will make you crave it regularly!
